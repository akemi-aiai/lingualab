The Magic Color Mix

Learn colors by mixing them! Target words: Blue, Yellow, Green.

Instructions: Take two glasses of water. Add blue paint to one, yellow to the other. Ask: "What happens if we mix?" Pour them together and shout "It's GREEN!"
