# January — Week 1 Homework

Welcome to your homework for Week 1!

## Reading Task
Read the short story “Snowy Day” and underline all winter vocabulary.

## Writing Task
Write 5 sentences using the verbs:
- play
- go
- like

## Listening Task
Listen to the audio about winter clothes and answer 5 questions.

## Creative Task
Draw your favourite winter activity and describe it with 3 sentences.
