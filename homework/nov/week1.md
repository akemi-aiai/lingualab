# Revision

## Vocabulary Review / 詞彙復習 (Workbook p.16)

Mix flashcards from Units 1 and 2. Cover them with a card with a hole. Ask your child to guess the word. Can do in groups or turn-taking.
Play “Get on the ride” (Revision 1) activity to review characters:
Tinky, Kelly, Mike, Henry, daddy, mommy, Miss Wang, a teacher.

Open e-book Revision 1 – Let’s Match:
Point to a word and let your child read.
Connect a picture incorrectly first (e.g., teacher → daddy) and ask: “daddy?”
Correct the answer together.
Let your child complete remaining workbook exercises. Check answers and give praise.

中文說明:
將第1、2單元的閃卡混合，遮住部分讓孩子猜單詞，可分組或輪流猜。
播放課件 Get on the ride 活動，複習角色：Tinky, Kelly, Mike, Henry, daddy, mommy, Miss Wang, a teacher。

打開課件或 E-Book 的 Revision 1 – Let’s Match：
指單詞讓孩子跟讀，先連錯誤答案再更正。
孩子完成練習冊剩餘練習，檢查並給予稱讚。

## Letter and Sound Review / 字母發音復習 (Workbook p.17)

Use Letters and Sounds flashcards to review Dd and Bb.
Play guessing game: show Dd → child guesses dog or daddy.
Play Dd & Bb songs:
Watch, sing, and clap hands along.
Repeat with karaoke version to reinforce memory.

Open Let’s Listen and Circle section:
First listen only, second time complete workbook exercise.
Check answers and give praise.
中文說明:
使用 Letters and Sounds 閃卡，復習字母 Dd、Bb 發音與單詞。
播放 Dd & Bb 歌謠，邊看邊唱，可拍手加強記憶。
打開 Let’s Listen and Circle 部分，第一次先聽，第二次在書上完成練習。



## Workbook Homework / 練習冊作業 (p.12–13)

Look at pictures, say the word mommy, tick the correct word. Complete remaining exercises.
Read short dialogue phrases, match with correct pictures.
Continue Look & Circle exercises: read the word, circle correct initial letter.


中文說明:
第12頁，看圖片說單詞 mommy，勾選正確單詞，完成其餘練習。
第13頁，朗讀短對話，將對話與正確圖片連線。
完成 Look & Circle 練習：讀單詞，圈出首字母。


## Mini Game / 小遊戲

Stand up and go to the back of the room.
Mix flashcards from Units 1 & 2.
Teacher shows a “ghost card” (e.g., a teacher).
Children run back when they see the card.
Show flashcards and ask: “What’s this?”
Correct answer → move forward one step.
Ghost card → teacher chases the slowest child.
Safety: ensure children don’t bump into each other.

中文說明:

站起來走到教室後方。
將第1、2單元閃卡混合，指定一張鬼閃卡（如 a teacher）。
孩子看到鬼閃卡快跑回後方。
展示閃卡並問：“What’s this?”
回答正確 → 前進一步。
鬼閃卡 → 老師追最慢的孩子。
注意安全，避免撞到。

## Learning Goals / 學習目標:

Review songs, vocabulary, action commands, and letter sounds from Units 1 & 2

Strengthen memory and recognition of words, letters, and phrases

Encourage participation, attention, and fun through games and songs

中文目標:

複習第1、2單元歌曲、詞彙、動作指令與字母發音

強化對單詞、字母及短語的記憶與辨識

透過遊戲和歌曲，培養孩子參與度、專注力及樂趣
