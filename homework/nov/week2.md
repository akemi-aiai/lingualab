# Unit 3: My friends

## Let’s Sing! / 一起唱歌 (Workbook p.18–19)

Prepare e-book or workbook pages. First, watch and listen to the video/song without distraction.
Play the song again. Sing along and add exaggerated actions:
“You’re a boy” → point to a boy
“Please stand here” → point to a spot

中文說明:
準備課件或練習冊頁面，先觀看並聽歌曲影片。
再次播放歌曲，跟唱並做動作：
“You’re a boy” → 指向男孩
“Please stand here” → 指向位置

Song Lyrics / 歌詞:

You’re a boy. Please stand here. You’re a girl. Please stand there.

## Let’s Listen! / 聽故事 (Workbook p.18–19)

Review main characters and greetings:

Point to Mike → “Hi, Mike” → child repeats

Point to Kelly → child says “Hi, Kelly”
