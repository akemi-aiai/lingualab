# Unit 2: My school

## Let’s Chant! (Workbook p.15) / 一起唱韻文


Play the audio from workbook. Watch and listen together.
Sing “book” → pick up a book
Sing “bag” → pick up a bag
Sing “a book in a bag” → put the book inside the bag

Use the sing-along version to let your child fill in missing words.
Play the audio and clap when they hear words starting with B.
If they forget to clap, ask them to say the word 3 times.


中文說明:

播放課件或練習冊的影片，觀看並聽。
再次播放，讓孩子跟唱，可加入動作：
唱到 book → 拿起一本書
唱到 bag → 拿起背包
唱到 a book in a bag → 把書放入背包
使用跟唱版，讓孩子填缺詞。
播放音檔，聽到 B 開頭單詞 拍手；忘拍就念單詞 3 次。


Let your child match letter puzzles with words starting with that letter.
Listen to audio and repeat the words.
Correct matches will connect the letter puzzle and word puzzle.
Practice both D and B, uppercase and lowercase, to help children distinguish letters.

中文說明:
讓孩子將字母拼圖與相應單詞拼圖配對。
聽錄音並跟讀單詞。
配對正確時，字母拼圖與單詞拼圖會結合。
練習字母 D 和 B，大寫和小寫，幫助孩子區分字母。

## Workbook Homework / 練習冊作業 (p.10–11)

Read the command “Open your book”, have your child do the action, then check the correct picture in the workbook. Continue with other commands.
Guide your child to read the letter B and related words.
Have your child circle words starting with B.

中文說明:
練習冊第10頁：老師讀指示語 “Open your book”，孩子做動作，並在練習冊上勾選正確圖片，繼續完成其他指示語練習。
練習冊第11頁：引導孩子朗讀字母 B 及單詞，圈出以字母 B 開頭的單詞。
