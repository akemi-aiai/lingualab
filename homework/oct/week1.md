# Unit 2: My school

Home Practice for Kids｜課後練習
Vocabulary / 單詞:

a teacher / 老師

a bag / 书包

a book / 书

## Let’s Sing! / 一起唱歌
Play the song Hello, Hello! and show actions: waving hand, placing hand on chest when saying your name.
Encourage your child to sing and do actions multiple times.

中文說明:
播放歌曲 Hello, Hello!，做動作：揮手、胸前放手介紹自己名字。
鼓勵孩子跟唱並做動作，多次練習。


## Parent Instructions / 家長說明

Encourage your child to stand, sing, and do the actions.
Repeat songs and commands to reinforce learning.

中文說明:
鼓勵孩子起立、唱歌、做動作。
重複歌曲和動作指令，加強記憶。
