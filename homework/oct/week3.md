# Unit 2: My school. Letter B

## Let’s Learn! Let’s Do! (Workbook p.14) / 一起學習，一起做
Say commands with big gestures: “Show me your book!”. Ask your child to stand up and follow the actions.

中文說明:
播放 Let’s Do! 錄音，誇張做動作：“Show me your book!”

Vocabulary / 單詞:
Open your book / 打開你的書
Show me your book / 給我看看你的書

## Let’s Say! (Workbook p.15) / 一起說

Say: “Look! Letter B”
Pronounce the sound: /b/, /b/, /b/
Play the audio with the picture of words. Let your child listen.
Play again and let your child repeat after you.
First pronounce /b/, then the words: bag, book
Use flashcards:
Show the letter B → child says /b/
Show pictures → child says bag or book

中文說明:
播放課件或練習冊主畫面，說：“Look! Letter B”
讀出發音：/b/, /b/, /b/
播放單詞錄音，讓孩子聽。
再播放一次，讓孩子跟讀：先 /b/，再說單詞 bag, book
使用閃卡：
指 B → 孩子說 /b/
指圖片 → 孩子說單詞 bag 或 book

## Letter B / 字母B

/b/ sound / 發音 /b/

bag / 小背包

book / 書
