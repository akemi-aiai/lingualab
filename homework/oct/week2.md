# Unit 2: My school

## Activity / 活動: Greetings and Self-Introduction

Stick a picture of yourself on the whiteboard and write your name and title.
Say: “Hello, I’m Miss (name).”
Approach your child and repeat: “Hello, I’m Miss (name).”
Encourage your child to respond: “Hello, Miss (name).”
Optional: shake hands or give a hug.

中文說明:
在白板上貼上老師畫像並寫上名字和稱謂。
說：“Hello, I’m Miss (名字)”。
走到孩子面前重複說：“Hello, I’m Miss (名字)”。
鼓勵孩子回答：“Hello, Miss (名字)”。
可握手或擁抱。

## Let’s Read! / 一起讀

Show a flashcard or a book: “Look, look!” Open and flip the book. Say “a book” and let the child repeat.
Ask the child to find a book in their backpack or on the table and give it to you, saying “a book”.
Repeat for “a bag”.
Stick character pictures on the board (Miss Wang, Mike, Kelly, Henry, Tinky). Say a name, e.g., “Kelly”, and have the child point or tap the correct picture while saying the name.

中文說明:
拿閃卡或書本說：“Look, look!” 打開翻閱書本，說 “a book”，讓孩子跟讀。
請孩子在書包或桌子上找一本書，說 “a book” 並交給老師。
用同樣方法教授 “a bag”。
在白板貼上角色圖片 (Miss Wang, Mike, Kelly, Henry, Tinky)，說名字如 “Kelly”，孩子拍正確圖片並說出名字。


## Let’s Talk! / 一起說話 (check the audio)

Review vocabulary with flashcards.
Introduce page 13, say “Look!”, and point to the pictures. Let children read sentences aloud.
let your child introduce themselves: “Hello/Hi, I’m (name).”

中文說明:
介紹第13頁圖片，說：“Look!” 指向圖片，引導孩子朗讀對話。
若有時間，孩子自由找朋友介紹自己，說：“Hello/Hi, I’m (名字)”。

## Homework / 作業 (Workbook 練習冊第 8–9 頁)

Connect Miss Wang’s picture to “a teacher”. Read aloud with your child. Let your child complete the rest of the exercises.

Ask “Who’s this?” Have your child answer and then repeat: “Hello, I’m (name).”

Maze activity: Match characters to the correct phrases.

中文說明:
練習冊第8頁，將 Miss Wang 連到 a teacher，帶孩子朗讀，完成其餘練習。
練習冊第9頁，問 “Who’s this?” 孩子回答後用短語 “Hello, I’m (名字)” 朗讀並完成迷宮遊戲。

## Mini Activity / 小遊戲

Give each child a card and some crayons.
Draw a self-portrait or favorite picture.
Pair up: children introduce themselves: “Hello, I’m (name).” Exchange cards with friends.

中文說明:

每個孩子拿卡片和彩筆。
畫自畫像或喜歡的圖案。
兩兩結對，自我介紹：“Hello, I’m (名字)”，然後交換名片。
